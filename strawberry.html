6 7 Strawberries

Strawberries are small, red, heart-shaped fruits with a sweet and slightly tart flavor. They are actually the enlarged end of the strawberry plant's stamen, with the small black spots (achenes) being the actual fruits. Strawberries are low-growing plants in the rose family and are known for their juicy texture and bright red color. They can be eaten fresh, used in desserts, or processed into jams and other products.
